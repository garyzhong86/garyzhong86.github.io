Motivations

  • Manged lane strategies can encourage new travel behaviors (car pooling) or promote adoption of new technologies (hybrid vehicles). 
  • The effectiveness of  CACC vehicles can be improved by providing priority or exclusive access to certain managed lane.
  • The traffic impact of various managed lane strategies have not been study at a corridor level.
  • A proper managed lane strategy could be crucial for the near-term deployment of CACC.  It can not only provide operational accommodation, but also incentives for the ownership of CACC-equipped vehicles.
